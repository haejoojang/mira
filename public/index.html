Mira sends you one private email when your cycle expects you. No app to install. No notifications on your phone. No data shared.

Tell us your last period date. We'll send a calendar view of what's likely next.

Most people fall between 21 and 35 days. Use 28 if you're not sure.
If you add a partner, they'll get a separate consent email first. They'll receive only fertile and ovulation alerts — never your period reminder.
